SAA-C03 — Domain 4: Design Cost-Optimized Architectures 領域 4：設計成本最佳化架構

AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate  |  Deep-Dive Study Guide  |  20% of Exam AWS 認證解決方案架構師 – 助理  |  深度學習指南  |  占考試 20%

1. Domain Overview 領域概覽

Domain 4 tests your ability to identify the most cost-effective AWS services and configurations without sacrificing reliability or performance. The exam focuses on matching the right pricing model to the workload pattern — choosing between Reserved, Spot, and Savings Plans for compute; selecting the correct S3 storage class; eliminating unnecessary data transfer charges; and knowing when serverless is cheaper than always-on infrastructure.

領域 4 測試您在不犧牲可靠性或效能的情況下識別最具成本效益 AWS 服務和設定的能力。考試重點在於為工作負載模式配對正確的定價模型——在預留、Spot 和節省方案之間為運算做選擇；選擇正確的 S3 儲存類別；消除不必要的資料傳輸費用；以及了解何時無伺服器比永久運行的基礎設施更便宜。

2. Key AWS Services 主要 AWS 服務

EC2 Pricing Models EC2 定價模型

On-Demand 隨需

  • Pay per second (Linux) or per hour (Windows); no commitment; highest unit cost 按秒計費（Linux）或按小時計費（Windows）；無承諾；最高單位成本
  • Exam rule: “unpredictable workload that cannot be interrupted” → On-Demand 考試規則：「無法預測且不能中斷的工作負載」→ 隨需

Reserved Instances (RI) 預留執行個體

  • 1-year or 3-year commitment; up to 72% discount vs. On-Demand 1 年或 3 年承諾；相較隨需最高 72% 折扣
  • Standard RI: highest discount; cannot change instance family, OS, or tenancy 標準預留執行個體：最高折扣；無法變更執行個體系列、作業系統或租用
  • Convertible RI: ~54% discount; can exchange for different instance family, OS, or tenancy 可轉換預留執行個體：約 54% 折扣；可兌換為不同執行個體系列、作業系統或租用
  • Payment options: All Upfront > Partial Upfront > No Upfront (deeper discount with more upfront) 付款選項：全部預付 > 部分預付 > 不預付（預付越多折扣越深）
  • RIs apply to any matching instance in the account — not tied to a specific instance ID 預留執行個體套用至帳號中任何相符的執行個體——不繫結至特定執行個體 ID
  • Exam rule: “steady-state, predictable 24/7 workload” → Reserved Instances; “need flexibility to change instance type” → Convertible RI 考試規則：「穩定狀態、可預測的 24/7 工作負載」→ 預留執行個體；「需要彈性變更執行個體類型」→ 可轉換預留執行個體

Savings Plans 節省方案

  • Commitment to a consistent dollar amount per hour for 1 or 3 years 承諾 1 年或 3 年的一致每小時美元金額
  • Compute Savings Plans: up to 66% discount; most flexible — any instance family, size, region, OS, tenancy; also covers Lambda and Fargate 運算節省方案：最高 66% 折扣；最彈性——任何執行個體系列、大小、區域、作業系統、租用；也涵蓋 Lambda 和 Fargate
  • EC2 Instance Savings Plans: up to 72% discount; locked to specific instance family in a specific region; flexible on size/OS within that family EC2 執行個體節省方案：最高 72% 折扣；鎖定特定區域中的特定執行個體系列；在該系列內大小/作業系統具彈性
  • Exam rule: “predictable spend, want flexibility across instance types and regions” → Compute Savings Plans; “specific instance family locked in” → EC2 Instance Savings Plans or Standard RI 考試規則：「可預測支出、希望跨執行個體類型和區域具彈性」→ 運算節省方案；「特定執行個體系列已鎖定」→ EC2 執行個體節省方案或標準預留執行個體

Spot Instances Spot 執行個體

  • Bid for spare AWS capacity; up to 90% discount; can be interrupted with 2-minute warning 競標 AWS 備用容量；最高 90% 折扣；可被中斷並有 2 分鐘警告
  • Spot Fleets: mix of Spot + On-Demand; allocation strategies: lowestPrice, diversified, capacityOptimized Spot 叢集：Spot + 隨需的混合；配置策略：最低價格、多樣化、容量最佳化
  • Use for: batch processing, big data, CI/CD, fault-tolerant stateless workloads, ML training 適用於：批次處理、大數據、CI/CD、容錯無狀態工作負載、ML 訓練
  • Exam rule: “fault-tolerant, flexible, interruptible workload” → Spot; “can’t tolerate interruption” → never pure Spot 考試規則：「容錯、彈性、可中斷的工作負載」→ Spot；「無法承受中斷」→ 絕不使用純 Spot

Dedicated Hosts 專用主機

  • Physical EC2 server dedicated to you; control over instance placement 專用於您的實體 EC2 伺服器；控制執行個體置放
  • Use for: BYOL software with per-socket or per-core licensing (SQL Server, Oracle, Windows Server) 適用於：具有按插槽或按核心授權的自帶授權（BYOL）軟體（SQL Server、Oracle、Windows Server）
  • Exam rule: “software licensing tied to physical cores/sockets” → Dedicated Host 考試規則：「軟體授權與實體核心/插槽掛鉤」→ 專用主機

Dedicated Instances 專用執行個體

  • Instances on single-tenant hardware; no control over physical placement; cheaper than Dedicated Hosts 在單租戶硬體上的執行個體；無法控制實體置放；比專用主機便宜
  • Exam rule: “compliance requiring no shared hardware, no BYOL needed” → Dedicated Instances 考試規則：「合規要求無共用硬體、不需要 BYOL」→ 專用執行個體

S3 Lifecycle Policies S3 生命週期政策

  • Transition actions: move objects to cheaper storage class after N days 轉換動作：N 天後將物件移至較便宜的儲存類別
  • Expiration actions: permanently delete objects after N days 到期動作：N 天後永久刪除物件
  • Early deletion fees apply: deleting before minimum storage duration still charges the full minimum 提前刪除費用適用：在最短儲存期前刪除仍會收取完整最低費用
  • Classic pattern: Standard (0d) → Standard-IA (30d) → Glacier Flexible (90d) → Deep Archive (180d) → Delete (365d+) 典型模式：標準（第 0 天）→ 標準-IA（第 30 天）→ Glacier 彈性（第 90 天）→ 深度封存（第 180 天）→ 刪除（第 365 天以上）
  • Exam rule: “logs accessed frequently for 30 days, rarely after” → lifecycle: Standard → Standard-IA at 30d → Glacier at 90d 考試規則：「日誌前 30 天頻繁存取，之後很少」→ 生命週期：標準 → 第 30 天轉換至標準-IA → 第 90 天轉換至 Glacier

Compute Cost Optimization 運算成本最佳化

Auto Scaling for Cost 用於成本的 Auto Scaling

  • Scale in during low-demand to terminate unneeded instances 低需求期間縮減以終止不需要的執行個體
  • Scheduled scaling: predictable traffic patterns (scale down nights/weekends) 排程擴展：可預測的流量模式（夜間/週末縮減）
  • Target tracking: maintain specific metric (50% CPU) — scales in when utilization drops 目標追蹤：維持特定指標（50% CPU）——使用率下降時縮減
  • Mixed instance policies: combine On-Demand base capacity with Spot for cost savings 混合執行個體政策：結合隨需基礎容量和 Spot 以節省成本

Lambda (Serverless) Lambda（無伺服器）

  • Pay per invocation ($0.20/million) and duration (per GB-second); zero cost when idle 按叫用計費（$0.20/百萬次）和持續時間（每 GB 秒）；閒置時零成本
  • Exam rule: “workload runs infrequently or in short bursts” → Lambda often cheaper than 24/7 EC2 考試規則：「工作負載不頻繁執行或短暫爆量」→ Lambda 通常比 24/7 EC2 便宜

Fargate

  • Pay per vCPU and memory per second; no EC2 to manage or pay for when idle 按每秒 vCPU 和記憶體計費；閒置時無需管理或為 EC2 付費
  • Exam rule: “containers with variable demand, minimize operational overhead” → Fargate 考試規則：「需求變動的容器、最小化營運負荷」→ Fargate

AWS Compute Optimizer AWS 運算最佳化器

  • ML-based rightsizing for EC2, EBS, Lambda, ECS on Fargate; requires 14 days minimum CloudWatch history EC2、EBS、Lambda、Fargate 上 ECS 的 ML 型資源調整；需要最少 14 天的 CloudWatch 歷程記錄
  • Exam rule: “identify over-provisioned EC2 instances with specific recommendations” → Compute Optimizer 考試規則：「識別具有特定建議的過度佈建 EC2 執行個體」→ 運算最佳化器

Storage Cost Optimization 儲存成本最佳化

EBS 彈性區塊存放區

  • Delete unattached EBS volumes (volumes persist after instance termination by default) 刪除未連接的 EBS 磁碟區（磁碟區預設在執行個體終止後仍會保留）
  • Switch gp2 → gp3: ~20% cheaper and higher baseline IOPS 從 gp2 切換至 gp3：便宜約 20%，且基準 IOPS 更高
  • Snapshots are incremental; delete old ones to reduce storage costs 快照是增量的；刪除舊快照以降低儲存成本

EFS 彈性檔案系統

  • EFS Lifecycle Management: automatically moves infrequently accessed files to EFS IA (~92% cheaper than EFS Standard) EFS 生命週期管理：自動將不頻繁存取的檔案移至 EFS IA（比 EFS 標準便宜約 92%）
  • Exam rule: “shared file system with mixed access patterns” → enable EFS Lifecycle Management 考試規則：「具有混合存取模式的共用檔案系統」→ 啟用 EFS 生命週期管理

Database Cost Optimization 資料庫成本最佳化

RDS

  • Reserved DB Instances: up to 69% discount for steady-state databases (1 or 3 year) 預留資料庫執行個體：穩定狀態資料庫可享最高 69% 折扣（1 年或 3 年）
  • Stop RDS instances during off-hours (dev/test): auto-restarts after 7 days 在非工作時間停止 RDS 執行個體（開發/測試）：7 天後自動重新啟動

Aurora

  • Aurora Serverless v1: scales to zero — truly zero cost when idle; for dev/test or very infrequent workloads Aurora Serverless v1：縮減至零——閒置時真正零成本；用於開發/測試或非常不頻繁的工作負載
  • Aurora Serverless v2: scales to near-zero in fine-grained increments; for variable production workloads Aurora Serverless v2：以細粒度增量縮減至接近零；用於變動的生產工作負載
  • Exam rule: “dev database used occasionally” → Aurora Serverless v1; “variable but non-zero production traffic” → Aurora Serverless v2 考試規則：「偶爾使用的開發資料庫」→ Aurora Serverless v1；「變動但非零的生產流量」→ Aurora Serverless v2

DynamoDB

  • On-demand: pay per request; no capacity planning; higher per-request cost; good for unpredictable traffic 隨需：按請求計費；無需容量規劃；每請求成本較高；適合無法預測的流量
  • Provisioned + auto scaling: lower per-unit cost; requires forecasting; use for stable traffic 佈建 + Auto Scaling：較低的每單位成本；需要預測；用於穩定流量
  • DynamoDB Reserved Capacity: 1 or 3 year commitment on provisioned capacity; up to 76% discount DynamoDB 預留容量：佈建容量的 1 年或 3 年承諾；最高 76% 折扣
  • Exam rule: “unknown or spiky DynamoDB traffic” → on-demand; “stable predictable traffic” → provisioned + reserved 考試規則：「未知或尖峰 DynamoDB 流量」→ 隨需；「穩定可預測流量」→ 佈建 + 預留

VPC Endpoints (cost impact) VPC 端點（成本影響）

  • Gateway Endpoints for S3 and DynamoDB: free; eliminates NAT Gateway data processing charges for that traffic S3 和 DynamoDB 的閘道端點：免費；消除該流量的 NAT 閘道資料處理費用

CloudFront (cost impact) CloudFront（成本影響）

  • CloudFront data transfer OUT pricing is lower than direct EC2/S3 data transfer OUT CloudFront 資料傳出定價低於直接 EC2/S3 資料傳出
  • Caching reduces origin requests → lower compute and data transfer costs at origin 快取減少來源請求 → 降低來源的運算和資料傳輸成本
  • Exam rule: “reduce data transfer costs for globally distributed content” → CloudFront reduces both latency and cost 考試規則：「降低全球分散式內容的資料傳輸成本」→ CloudFront 同時降低延遲和成本

Minimize Cross-AZ Traffic 最小化跨可用區域流量

  • Colocate resources in the same AZ (read replicas, ElastiCache nodes near the app tier) 在同一可用區域共置資源（唯讀複本、靠近應用程式層的 ElastiCache 節點）
  • Exam rule: “reduce data transfer costs within a region” → minimize cross-AZ traffic by colocating resources 考試規則：「降低區域內的資料傳輸成本」→ 透過共置資源最小化跨可用區域流量

Cost Monitoring & Governance 成本監控與治理

AWS Cost Explorer AWS Cost Explorer（成本探索器）

  • Visualize and analyze historical costs; 12 months history + 12 months forecast 視覺化和分析歷史成本；12 個月歷程記錄 + 12 個月預測
  • Rightsizing recommendations for underutilized EC2 instances 未充分利用的 EC2 執行個體的資源調整建議
  • Exam rule: “analyze past spending trends and forecast future costs” → Cost Explorer 考試規則：「分析過去的支出趨勢和預測未來成本」→ Cost Explorer

AWS Budgets AWS 預算

  • Set cost, usage, RI utilization, Savings Plan coverage budgets with email/SNS alerts 設定成本、使用量、預留執行個體利用率、節省方案涵蓋率預算，搭配電子郵件/SNS 警示
  • Can trigger automated actions (e.g., apply SCP when budget exceeded) 可觸發自動化動作（例如，超出預算時套用 SCP）
  • Exam rule: “alert the team when monthly spend exceeds $X” → AWS Budgets 考試規則：「每月支出超過 $X 時警示團隊」→ AWS 預算

AWS Cost and Usage Report (CUR) AWS 成本和使用量報告

  • Most granular billing data; line-item detail for every service and resource 最細粒度的帳單資料；每個服務和資源的明細項目詳情
  • Exports to S3; query with Athena or load into Redshift 匯出至 S3；使用 Athena 查詢或載入 Redshift
  • Exam rule: “detailed cost allocation for chargeback across teams” → CUR 考試規則：「跨團隊費用分攤的詳細成本分配」→ 成本和使用量報告

AWS Trusted Advisor AWS 受信任的顧問

  • Automated best practice checks: idle EC2, unassociated EIPs, underutilized RDS, idle load balancers 自動化最佳實務檢查：閒置 EC2、未關聯的 EIP、使用率不足的 RDS、閒置負載平衡器
  • Full checks require Business or Enterprise support plan 完整檢查需要商業或企業支援方案
  • Exam rule: “quickly identify idle or underutilized resources” → Trusted Advisor 考試規則：「快速識別閒置或使用率不足的資源」→ Trusted Advisor

AWS Compute Optimizer AWS 運算最佳化器

  • ML-based rightsizing for EC2, EBS, Lambda, ECS; requires 14+ days of CloudWatch metrics EC2、EBS、Lambda、ECS 的 ML 型資源調整；需要 14 天以上的 CloudWatch 指標
  • Exam rule: “specific rightsizing recommendations for EC2 instance types” → Compute Optimizer (more detailed than Trusted Advisor) 考試規則：「EC2 執行個體類型的特定資源調整建議」→ 運算最佳化器（比 Trusted Advisor 更詳細）

3. Architecture Patterns 架構模式

Pattern 1: Cost-Optimized Web Tier with Mixed Instances 模式 1：使用混合執行個體的成本最佳化 Web 層

Services: ALB → ASG (On-Demand base + Spot for burst) + Reserved EC2 for baseline

服務：ALB → ASG（隨需基礎 + Spot 用於爆量）+ 預留 EC2 用於基準

Why it works: Reserved Instances cover the always-on baseline at deep discount. Spot covers burst traffic at 70–90% off. On-Demand handles any Spot interruption gap. Total cost dramatically lower than all On-Demand.

為何有效：預留執行個體以深度折扣涵蓋永久運行的基準。Spot 以 70–90% 折扣涵蓋爆量流量。隨需處理任何 Spot 中斷缺口。總成本遠低於全部使用隨需。

Exam signal: “web application with variable traffic, minimize cost” → ASG with On-Demand base + Spot for burst, Reserved for baseline.

考試信號：「具變動流量的 Web 應用程式、最小化成本」→ ASG 搭配隨需基礎 + Spot 用於爆量，預留用於基準。

Pattern 2: S3 Cost Optimization with Lifecycle 模式 2：使用生命週期的 S3 成本最佳化

Services: S3 Standard → Lifecycle Policy → Standard-IA → Glacier Flexible → Deep Archive → Expiration

服務：S3 標準 → 生命週期政策 → 標準-IA → Glacier 彈性 → 深度封存 → 到期

Why it works: Objects automatically move to cheaper tiers as they age. Active data stays in Standard; aging data transitions automatically with no application changes needed.

為何有效：物件隨著老化自動移至較便宜的層。活躍資料保留在標準層；老化資料自動轉換，無需任何應用程式變更。

Exam signal: “large volume of log files accessed frequently for 30 days, rarely after” → lifecycle: Standard → Standard-IA at 30d → Glacier at 90d.

考試信號：「大量日誌檔案前 30 天頻繁存取，之後很少」→ 生命週期：標準 → 第 30 天轉換至標準-IA → 第 90 天轉換至 Glacier。

Pattern 3: Serverless to Eliminate Idle Cost 模式 3：無伺服器以消除閒置成本

Services: API Gateway → Lambda → DynamoDB on-demand

服務：API Gateway → Lambda → DynamoDB 隨需

Why it works: No resources running (and billing) when there are no requests. Pay only for actual invocations. Perfect for irregular traffic or low-volume APIs.

為何有效：無請求時沒有資源執行（也不計費）。只為實際叫用付費。完美適用於不規則流量或低流量 API。

Exam signal: “API used a few hours per day”, “development environment”, “bursty infrequent workload” → serverless instead of always-on EC2 + RDS.

考試信號：「每天使用幾小時的 API」、「開發環境」、「突發且不頻繁的工作負載」→ 無伺服器而非永久運行的 EC2 + RDS。

Pattern 4: Reduce NAT Gateway Costs 模式 4：降低 NAT 閘道成本

Services: Private EC2 → S3 Gateway Endpoint (S3 traffic) + NAT Gateway (everything else)

服務：私有 EC2 → S3 閘道端點（S3 流量）+ NAT 閘道（其他所有流量）

Why it works: S3 Gateway Endpoint is free and routes S3 traffic within the AWS network, bypassing NAT Gateway entirely. Eliminates NAT data processing fees for S3 — often the largest NAT cost driver.

為何有效：S3 閘道端點免費，在 AWS 網路內路由 S3 流量，完全繞過 NAT 閘道。消除 S3 的 NAT 資料處理費用——通常是最大的 NAT 成本驅動因素。

Exam signal: “high NAT Gateway costs; EC2 frequently reads from S3 via NAT” → add free S3 Gateway Endpoint.

考試信號：「NAT 閘道費用高；EC2 頻繁透過 NAT 從 S3 讀取」→ 新增免費 S3 閘道端點。

Pattern 5: Cost-Optimized Data Analytics 模式 5：成本最佳化資料分析

Services: S3 (raw data in Parquet, partitioned) → Athena (query-on-demand) → QuickSight

服務：S3（Parquet 格式的原始資料、已分割）→ Athena（隨需查詢）→ QuickSight

Why it works: No always-on data warehouse. Athena charges per TB scanned ($5/TB). Columnar formats (Parquet, ORC) + S3 partitioning reduce scan size and cost by 30–90%.

為何有效：無永久運行的資料倉儲。Athena 按掃描 TB 計費（$5/TB）。欄式格式（Parquet、ORC）+ S3 分割可將掃描大小和成本降低 30–90%。

Exam signal: “infrequent analytical queries on large datasets in S3” → Athena instead of Redshift; use Parquet + partitioning.

考試信號：「S3 中大型資料集的不頻繁分析查詢」→ Athena 而非 Redshift；使用 Parquet + 分割。

5. Exam Traps & Gotchas 考試陷阱與注意事項

  1. Spot Instances are not suitable for workloads that can’t tolerate interruption. The exam will offer Spot as the cheapest answer for a production database or a “critical” workload. These are always wrong. Spot = fault-tolerant + interruptible only. Spot 執行個體不適合無法承受中斷的工作負載。考試會為生產資料庫或「關鍵」工作負載提供 Spot 作為最便宜的答案。這些始終是錯誤的。Spot = 僅限容錯 + 可中斷。
  2. Minimum storage duration fees apply even if you delete early. Delete an object from S3 Standard-IA after 10 days and you still pay for 30 days. Glacier minimum is 90 days; Deep Archive is 180 days. Frequently changing data in Glacier is very expensive. 即使提前刪除，最短儲存期費用仍適用。10 天後從 S3 標準-IA 刪除物件，您仍需為 30 天付費。Glacier 最低為 90 天；深度封存為 180 天。在 Glacier 中頻繁變更的資料非常昂貴。
  3. S3 Intelligent-Tiering has a monitoring fee per object. For large numbers of tiny objects (<128 KB), the monitoring cost can exceed the storage savings. Not always the cheapest option — a manual lifecycle policy may be better for known access patterns. S3 智慧型分層每個物件有監控費用。對於大量微小物件（<128 KB），監控成本可能超過儲存節省。並非始終最便宜的選項——對於已知存取模式，手動生命週期政策可能更好。
  4. Data transfer between AZs costs money (~$0.01/GB each way). Placing ElastiCache or a read replica in a different AZ from the app generates charges on every request. Fix: colocate resources in the same AZ. 可用區域間的資料傳輸需要費用（約 $0.01/GB 每個方向）。將 ElastiCache 或唯讀複本置於與應用程式不同的可用區域，每個請求都會產生費用。解決方法：在同一可用區域共置資源。
  5. NAT Gateway charges for ALL outbound traffic, including S3. Private EC2 instances making frequent S3 requests through NAT Gateway incur data processing fees on every byte. A free S3 Gateway Endpoint eliminates this entirely. NAT 閘道對所有輸出流量收費，包括 S3。透過 NAT 閘道頻繁發出 S3 請求的私有 EC2 執行個體，每個位元組都會產生資料處理費用。免費的 S3 閘道端點完全消除這個問題。
  6. Reserved Instances apply to account-level usage, not a specific instance ID. If you buy an RI for m5.large and run multiple m5.large instances, the discount applies to one automatically. You don’t lose the RI if you terminate a specific instance. 預留執行個體套用至帳號層級使用量，而非特定執行個體 ID。如果您為 m5.large 購買預留執行個體並執行多個 m5.large 執行個體，折扣自動套用至其中一個。終止特定執行個體不會失去預留執行個體。
  7. Savings Plans apply after Reserved Instances. AWS applies RI discounts first, then Savings Plans to remaining On-Demand usage. They stack, not conflict. 節省方案在預留執行個體之後套用。AWS 先套用預留執行個體折扣，然後將節省方案套用至剩餘的隨需使用量。它們疊加，而不是衝突。
  8. Stopping RDS doesn’t eliminate all costs. Storage costs continue. After 7 days, a stopped RDS instance auto-restarts. For extended dev/test downtime, snapshot → delete → restore is cheaper. 停止 RDS 不會消除所有成本。儲存成本持續。7 天後，已停止的 RDS 執行個體自動重新啟動。對於較長的開發/測試停機時間，快照 → 刪除 → 還原更便宜。

Question 1 — A company runs a fleet of EC2 instances for a video rendering pipeline. Jobs are submitted throughout the day and take 30–60 minutes to complete. Jobs can be restarted if interrupted. The company wants to minimize compute costs. What should the solutions architect recommend? 問題 1——某公司為視訊渲染管道執行一批 EC2 執行個體。工作全天提交，需要 30–60 分鐘完成。工作被中斷時可以重新啟動。公司希望最小化運算成本。解決方案架構師應建議什麼？

  2. Use Reserved Instances with a 1-year commitment 使用具有 1 年承諾的預留執行個體
  3. Use Spot Instances with a Spot Fleet configured with the diversified allocation strategy 使用 Spot 執行個體搭配配置了多樣化配置策略的 Spot 叢集
  4. Use Dedicated Hosts to maximize physical server utilization 使用專用主機以最大化實體伺服器利用率
Answer: C 答案：C
  • C is correct: Video rendering is a classic Spot workload — batch, fault-tolerant, restartable. Spot offers up to 90% discount. The diversified strategy distributes across multiple instance pools to reduce interruption risk. C 正確：視訊渲染是典型的 Spot 工作負載——批次、容錯、可重新啟動。Spot 提供最高 90% 折扣。多樣化策略跨多個執行個體集區分佈以降低中斷風險。
  • A is wrong: On-Demand is the most expensive option with no cost optimization benefit for this workload. A 錯誤：隨需是最昂貴的選項，對此工作負載沒有成本最佳化優勢。
  • B is wrong: Reserved Instances are for steady 24/7 workloads, not variable batch jobs. Less discount than Spot and wastes capacity when pipeline is idle. B 錯誤：預留執行個體用於穩定的 24/7 工作負載，而非變動的批次作業。折扣少於 Spot，且管道閒置時浪費容量。
  • D is wrong: Dedicated Hosts are the most expensive option and exist for BYOL licensing compliance, not cost optimization. D 錯誤：專用主機是最昂貴的選項，存在是為了 BYOL 授權合規，而非成本最佳化。

  1. Transition to S3 Glacier Deep Archive after 30 days; expire after 365 days 30 天後轉換至 S3 Glacier 深度封存；365 天後到期
  2. Transition to S3 Standard-IA after 30 days, to S3 Glacier Flexible Retrieval after 90 days; expire after 365 days 30 天後轉換至 S3 標準-IA，90 天後轉換至 S3 Glacier 彈性擷取；365 天後到期

  • B is correct: Matches the access pattern exactly. Standard for days 0–30 (frequent). Standard-IA for days 30–90 (occasional, fast retrieval). Glacier Flexible for days 90–365 (rarely accessed, low cost). Expires at 1 year. B 正確：完全符合存取模式。第 0–30 天為標準（頻繁）。第 30–90 天為標準-IA（偶爾、快速擷取）。第 90–365 天為 Glacier 彈性（很少存取、低成本）。1 年後到期。
  • A is wrong: Moving to Deep Archive at day 30 means 12-hour retrieval times for logs still occasionally needed. Mismatches the access pattern. A 錯誤：第 30 天移至深度封存意味著仍偶爾需要的日誌需要 12 小時擷取時間。與存取模式不符。

The team wants to minimize infrastructure costs. What should the solutions architect recommend? 問題 3——新創公司在 ALB 後方的 EC2 執行個體上執行 REST API。每天 20 小時流量幾乎為零，但在上班時間的 4 小時內顯著激增。API 是無狀態的。團隊希望最小化基礎設施成本。解決方案架構師應建議什麼？
